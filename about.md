# 20+ Years of Excellence in Zoo Mesh & Aviary Netting

![Zoo Mesh Product](/images/zoo-fencing-mesh.jpg)

Hebmetalmesh is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high‑quality zoo mesh and aviary netting, with over two decades of dedicated experience in the animal enclosure industry.

Our expertise lies in designing and producing durable, flexible, and safe wire mesh solutions for zoos, wildlife parks, bird sanctuaries, and animal shelters worldwide.

## Why Choose Our Zoo Mesh?

- **20+ Years of Industry Expertise** – Trusted by zoos and wildlife professionals globally.
- **Superior Material** – Made from hardwood stainless steel wire rope, hand‑woven with a rhombus mesh pattern for exceptional strength and flexibility.
- **Long‑Lasting Durability** – Resistant to breakage, corrosion, and extreme weather – designed for a service life of over 30 years.
- **Customizable** – Mesh openings and sheet sizes can be tailored to your specific project requirements. We also cut rolls to your frame dimensions at no extra cost.
- **Factory Direct Pricing** – Competitive wholesale rates with no middlemen.

## Our Products Are Ideal For:
- Zoo animal enclosures
- Aviary netting for birds
- Wildlife habitats and sanctuaries
- Outdoor pet enclosures
